What’s in The Coffee Can?

The Coffee Can has been operating in Cairns for four years now and are the only consistently eco-friendly coffee van. We have a Cafe in Redlynch which we bake cakes and we also have a Coffee Cart which is used for corporate events, weddings and indoor venues.

Because we care about you, your family and our planet, we only use the very best coffee that is ethically sourced and locally roasted by the White Whale Coffee Roasters. A percentage of profits from every purchase of beans we make now goes to The Reef Restoration Foundation to assist them in establishing more ocean-based coral nurseries that regenerate coral on the Great Barrier Reef.

The Coffee Can has serviced events with up to 10,000 people attending, nothing is too big or small if we have the availability.  We are also able to cater for your work meetings, Expo’s,  Birthdays and Special Occasions from our Cafe in Redlynch.

We only use Mungalli milk, from locally farmed cows on the Tablelands, our Syrups for coffee and frappes are from a family owned Australian company and contain no artificial colours and flavours. Our cups and lids are biodegradable and compostable with lids and lining made from plant materials, not plastic and we use only paper straws.

A percentage of profits from every purchase of beans we make goes to The Reef Restoration Foundation to assist in establishing ocean-based coral nurseries that regenerate coral on the Great Barrier Reef.

We are passionate about coffee, supporting other local businesses and great customer service. Recently, we were asked to provide a coffee service for the Cairns Regional Council offices whilst their own Cafe took some time off over Christmas, after some great feedback, we have been asked to go back next year.
