Locals Supporting Locals

Join us for a different culinary experience every Friday.
Dine in or takeaway, Cafe is open for drinks and dessert.
BYO wine or beer.

Homemade and tasty, baked on site or by some of Cairns’ best home bakers. Only the best local ingredients used wherever possible and locals supporting locals. Enjoy our specialty coffee, lovingly prepared by our friendly staff. We also stock only the best daily basics:

Brumby’s Bread, Mungalli Milk, Mungalli Eggs and Newspapers.

Come and chill out with us!
